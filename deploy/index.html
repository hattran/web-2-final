Overview

Wizardry and wonder fill the air each October when Chestnut Hill transforms for Witches and Wizards Weekend.

Formerly the Harry Potter Festival, the Witches and Wizards festival weekend takes over the charming neighborhood for spellbinding events that morph the village into an enchanting magic-filled haunt.

This two-day event starts a new tradition that combines ghosts of wizards-past and fun activities for the entire family across multiple locations in Chestnut Hill.

Older witches and wizards can enjoy a Brews & Broomsticks pub crawl through outdoor beer gardens and have their fortunes told along the way.
